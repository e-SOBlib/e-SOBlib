About Us

e-SOBlib — Powered by Students

Welcome to e-SOBlib — a digital platform created and managed by students of the State University of Zanzibar (SUZA), Chwaka Campus, School of Business, pursuing a Bachelor Degree in Information Technology with Accounting.

While we proudly serve the SOB Chwaka Campus community, e-SOBlib is an independent academic resource and not an official SUZA university service, we respect all SUZA intellectual property. We charge a small fee to keep our servers running. For more details on our legal policies please visit our Terms of Service.

The platform focuses mainly on business-related subjects. Our goal is to make learning easier by providing instant access to curated academic notes and exam preparation materials.

Our Mission: To empower students through accessible, high-quality learning resources and a user-friendly digital library experience.
